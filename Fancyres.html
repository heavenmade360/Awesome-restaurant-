Notre Histoire

A Legacy of
Exquisite Craft

Founded in 1997 by the Mulozela family, Maison Éclipse was born from a singular obsession — to transform the finest seasonal produce into moments of pure transcendence. Nestled in the heart of the 8th arrondissement, our dining room has long been a sanctuary for those who believe a meal can alter one's perception of the world.

Over the years, we have grown into one of Lusaka’s most respected fine-dining restaurants — known for excellent service, carefully prepared dishes, and a relaxed but elegant atmosphere.

We work with trusted suppliers both locally and internationally to bring the best ingredients to your table. From fresh seafood and premium meats to seasonal vegetables and speciality produce, every dish is prepared with care and attention to detail.

Expérience

Every Detail
Considered

From the moment you step beneath our discreet lantern on Rue du Faubourg Saint-Honoré, you enter a world where every element — the silk damask chairs, the handblown Baccarat crystal, the temperature of the room — has been calibrated for a single purpose: your complete ease.

Our sommelier, trained at the Académie du Vin in Bordeaux, curates a cellar of 1,200 references spanning five decades. Our service team is schooled in the French tradition of invisible attentiveness — present when needed, imperceptible when not.

  • Private dining rooms for 6–18 guests
  • Tasting menus with 7 to 12 courses
  • Wine pairing experiences
  • Chef's table with full kitchen view
